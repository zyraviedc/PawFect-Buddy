My Personal Information

Hi! I’m Zyra Vie S. Del Castillo, a student at the College of Information and Computing Studies at Northern Iloilo State University, Estancia Campus. Beyond my passion for technology and innovation, I find true peace and happiness in the company of my beloved dogs.

Growing up in a household where dogs were always part of the family, I’ve learned firsthand the incredible bond that forms between humans and their furry companions. Dogs are so much more than pets—they are loyal, loving, and intuitive creatures that offer unconditional companionship and teach us valuable life lessons about responsibility, empathy, and joy.

I’ve always been fascinated by how dogs can truly adapt to their owners’ lifestyles, whether it’s a high-energy pup that’s ready for long walks or a calm, laid-back dog who loves lounging around the house. Over the years, my love for dogs has only grown stronger, and I’ve come to realize how having a furry friend can improve mental health, increase physical activity, and provide an endless source of comfort and happiness.

Through this platform, I aim to share my experiences and knowledge about dog ownership—everything from the basics of choosing the right breed for your lifestyle to the emotional and physical benefits of having a dog in your life. Whether you’re a seasoned dog owner or someone considering adopting a dog for the first time, I hope to offer helpful tips, heartwarming stories, and insights on how these wonderful animals can truly become a cherished part of your family..

Having a dog is a life-changing experience, and I want to help others discover the joys and rewards that come with having a furry friend by their side.
